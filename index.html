Why Classly?

Have you ever wanted to know how to get to your classes? Wanted to connect with others in your courses? Classly makes this all possible by bringing together the community and the school.

Features

  • Course Search
  • Find My Class
  • Classroom Connections
  • Class Chat
  • Event Finder
  • Campus Interests
  • Ask for field-specific help
  • Food hunter
    • Crawls the UNH Dining Hall menus for certain "hot topic" food items and lets you know when they're coming next!

About Us

Classly was created by a group of students at the University of New Hampshire Interoperability Lab. It started as a hackathon project, but we quickly built it into something more. We wanted to create a platform that would help students connect with each other and their school. We hope you enjoy using Classly as much as we enjoyed creating it.
